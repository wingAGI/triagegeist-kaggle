# Cover Brief

## Concept

An emergency department triage board with one patient card highlighted in red as a potential undertriage risk.

## Visual Elements

- Left: incoming patient summary card with abnormal vitals
- Center: acuity scale from 1 to 5
- Right: alert panel reading `Potential undertriage risk`
- Background: emergency department monitor lines and muted hospital palette

## Style

- Clean clinical interface
- White, slate, and emergency red accents
- Serious and research-oriented, not startup-marketing

## Overlay Text

`AI-Assisted Emergency Triage`
